About Me

I’m Juraij Bin Abdul Khadir, a passionate Flutter developer with over 1 year of hands-on experience building cross-platform mobile applications. My journey began with a curiosity for crafting clean, responsive, and efficient UIs — and quickly grew into a commitment to building user-focused apps that solve real problems. I’ve developed several personal and client-side apps using Flutter, integrating technologies like Firebase, Supabase, and REST APIs. I’m skilled in state management (Provider), UI/UX design using Figma, and incorporating tools like Postman, Git, and Android Studio in my workflow. What sets me apart is my ability to take an idea from concept to deployment — handling UI design, backend integration, and testing with a clean, maintainable codebase. I enjoy working on solo projects but am equally excited to collaborate with teams and continuously learn new skills in mobile app development. Whether it's a minimalistic expense tracker, a joke generator app, or a more complex business app, I aim to deliver fast, elegant, and scalable solutions.

Project Tracky

Tracky may sound cheesy, but it's serious about your spending! This is a simple, minimalistic expense tracker built entirely with Flutter. Designed for speed and ease, Tracky helps you keep an eye on your daily expenses without the clutter.
No logins, no credentials — just open and start tracking. Whether you're budgeting your day or just want a record of where your money goes, Tracky keeps it clean and convenient.
🎯 Features:
💸 Add and manage daily expenses effortlessly
📊 Minimal and intuitive UI — no distractions
🔓 Open access — no sign-up or login needed
⚡ Fast, lightweight, and perfect for quick tracking
🎯 Ideal for personal use or testing Flutter concepts
Whether you're managing coffee runs or monthly budgets, Tracky helps you stay on track — no strings attached.

Just Jokes
